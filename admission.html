Admissions - Welcome

Welcome to the Debugging University of Technology (Debugtech), Nigeria's first private University of Technology and one of Nigeria's leading private Universities.

Through this website we hope to give you a comprehensive experience of the academic and student life at Debugtech; and an idea of what to expect, should you decide to pursue a degree programme with us.

Debugtech is especially proud of its nationally and regionally acclaimed academic record.

We are distinct from other tertiary institutions in that, Debugtech is home to a breed of well seasoned academics, who have left landmarks in the sands of time, in Nigeria, Africa and beyond.

Hence our students are trained by outstanding academic leaders in their various fields.

Our aim is to prepare leaders for the future.

Debugtech therefore takes a holistic approach to the development of the student, ensuring that you receive qualitative education that goes beyond the classroom curriculum.

There are many opportunities to develop your leadership skills, through participation in the different clubs and societies on campus, as well as engaging in various' University committees and student governance within the auspices of the Debugtech University Students' Association.

At Debugtech, you also have the privilege of studying in a dynamic and multicultural environment with staff and students from all over Nigeria, West Africa and the rest of the world.

We are keen about attracting talented and committed students and we have many programmes and different options in terms of academic work, socially inclusive programmes and scholarship bursaries.

To this end, Debugtech welcomes applicants from all over Nigeria and the world. Of a truth, making the choice of which University to attend is one very important phase of life, and as such, we welcome your interest in Debugtech University of Technology.

On admission to Debugtech, you do not just become a student, you become a part of a unique, distinguished and renowned group of people, who are committed to enrolling, developing and producing a community of learners, leaders and thinkers, whose education will be enhanced by diverse experiences in preparation to serve in all spheres of life and society.

The University offers various undergraduate degree programmes and we invite you to catch in on this opportunity to improve your lots!
